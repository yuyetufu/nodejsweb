About Us

Founded in 2010, the Green Harmony Foundation is a leading international non-profit environmental organization. We partner with global stakeholders to advance climate action, forest conservation, and ocean ecosystem restoration, implementing hundreds of projects in over 50 countries and impacting hundreds of millions of lives.

Forest Conservation

Restoring tropical rainforests and planting over 10 million trees worldwide.

Ocean Ecosystems

Cleaning marine plastics and protecting coral reefs and marine life.

Climate Action

Promoting renewable energy transitions and reducing carbon emissions.
